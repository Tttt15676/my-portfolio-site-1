Who We Are

Spotless Shine was created in 2022 with a simple but powerful mission: to give people more time, less stress, and the comfort of a clean, welcoming home or workspace. Founded by a working mom who knows the struggle of balancing life and cleanliness, our company is built on reliability, attention to detail, and pride in every job we take on.

We’re not a faceless franchise — we’re a passionate, local cleaning team that treats every client like a neighbor. We understand that letting someone into your home is personal, and that’s why we put trust, consistency, and respect at the center of what we do. Our cleaners are background-checked, trained, and genuinely care about delivering results that shine.

Whether you need a one-time deep clean, regular upkeep, or help preparing for a move, Spotless Shine is here to help. We don’t just clean — we care. Every home and office has a story, and we’re honored to play a small role in keeping yours healthy, happy, and sparkling clean.
